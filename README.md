# Coffee Brand Concepts

Five brand directions for a Chinese-inspired specialty coffee brand targeting the US market.

## Brand Concepts

1. **红墨 Red Ink** - Traditional brush strokes, ink wash aesthetic, bold red and white calligraphy
2. **东方 Eastern** - 1960s-70s Cultural Revolution propaganda style, bold reds and yellows
3. **玉露 Jade Dew** - Modern minimalist, jade green, clean contemporary Chinese
4. **麻辣 Má Là** - Playful Gen Z, bold colors, irreverent, meme-ready
5. **老上海 Old Shanghai** - 1920s-30s Art Deco, Jazz Age glamour, vintage sophistication
