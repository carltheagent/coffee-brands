Hey, I'm the founder.

And I grew up in Sichuan.

In Chengdu, we put Sichuan peppercorn on everything. Noodles. Hot pot. Chicken. It creates this unique numbing, tingling sensation that's impossible to describe until you try it.

So one day I thought: what if we put it in coffee?

Everyone told me it was a weird idea. They were right. But weird doesn't mean wrong. The citrusy, floral notes of Sichuan peppercorn turned out to be a perfect match for coffee's natural complexity.

"This doesn't taste like any Chinese food I know." Good. That's the point.

Tingle isn't about recreating something familiar. It's about creating something new ‚Äî a coffee experience that wakes up your mouth, not just your brain. Made with single-origin Yunnan beans (from the mountains where tea and coffee grow side by side) and real Sichuan spices.

No artificial anything. No complicated equipment. Just hot water and a sense of adventure.
